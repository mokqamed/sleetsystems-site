SMS Messaging Disclosure

Effective: April 30, 2026

What you'll receive

If you opt in at the point of sale of a SleetPOS-enabled merchant, you may receive: (a) transactional messages such as digital receipts, refund confirmations, and loyalty point updates, and (b) if you separately opt in to marketing, promotional messages such as discounts and new-product announcements.

Frequency

Transactional messages are sent at the time of each qualifying transaction. Marketing message frequency varies by merchant and is typically 0–4 messages per month.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, charged by your mobile carrier. Sleet Systems does not charge End Customers for SMS.

How to stop

Reply STOP to any message at any time to opt out of all SMS — both transactional and marketing — from that merchant. Reply HELP to receive contact information.

Privacy

Phone numbers and consent records are handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your phone number to third parties.

Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Service may not be available on all carriers.
