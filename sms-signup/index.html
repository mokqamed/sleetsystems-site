SMS Sign-Up

Opt in to receive text messages from a SleetPOS-enabled merchant — digital receipts, order updates, loyalty rewards, and occasional promotions. SMS is entirely optional. Receipts are also always available on paper or by email; no purchase or service requires SMS consent.

By submitting, you confirm the above and acknowledge our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

You're signed up. You'll receive a confirmation text shortly. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help.

Program details

Program name: SleetPOS SMS

What you'll receive: Transactional notifications (receipts, order confirmations, loyalty updates) and merchant-initiated promotional messages.

Frequency: Transactional messages are sent per qualifying purchase. Promotional messages are typically 0–4 per month per merchant.

Cost: Message and data rates may apply, charged by your mobile carrier. Sleet Systems does not charge for SMS.

How to stop: Reply STOP to any message to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

Support: developer@sleetsystems.com

You may also sign up in person at the point of sale of any participating SleetPOS merchant by providing your mobile number and giving verbal or on-screen consent.
