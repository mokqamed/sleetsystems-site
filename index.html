How we build

The principles behind our software.

A small set of beliefs that guide everything we ship.

Trust by default

Every transaction is hash-chained for cryptographic integrity. Append-only ledgers mean audit-ready records, every day, without effort.

Speed at the counter

Every interaction is built for the busiest minute of the busiest hour. If it doesn't make a cashier faster, it doesn't ship.

Compliant communication

Customer messaging is opt-in, auditable, and revocable. STOP keywords work. Quiet hours are honored. The audit trail is complete.

No lock-in

Hardware-agnostic. Processor-agnostic. Your data is yours. Switching off our software should be as fair as switching to it was.

About Sleet Systems

Sleet Systems Inc is a Corporation registered in the State of New York, headquartered in Yonkers. We are an independent software vendor (ISV) — we build and license software, we do not sell hardware.

We work with retailers who own their own terminals, peripherals, and customer relationships, and want software that respects all three. Today our flagship product is SleetPOS. Tomorrow there will be more.

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 30, 2026

Sleet Systems Inc ("Sleet Systems," "we," "our," or "us") operates SleetPOS, point-of-sale software licensed to retail merchants ("Merchants") who use it to process transactions and communicate with their own customers ("End Customers"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when Merchants and End Customers interact with our products and services.

Information We Collect

From Merchants: business name, owner name, email address, phone number, business address, EIN, payment processor credentials, and store configuration data.

From End Customers (collected by Merchants and processed on their behalf): name, phone number, email address, transaction history, loyalty data, and SMS/email consent records.

Automatically: device identifiers, IP address, app version, error logs, and usage analytics necessary to operate and secure the service.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our products
  • To process transactions on behalf of Merchants
  • To deliver SMS and email messages that End Customers have opted in to receive
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and applicable law (CAN-SPAM, TCPA, A2P 10DLC compliance)
  • To communicate with Merchants about their account and our service

Sharing

We do not sell End Customer personal information. We share data only with: (a) service providers under contract who help us deliver the service (Twilio for SMS, Resend for email, Google Firebase for hosting and authentication), (b) authorities when legally required, and (c) successors in the event of a corporate transaction.

Your Rights

End Customers may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting the Merchant they transacted with, or by emailing sleetpostech@gmail.com. Merchants honor these requests through tooling we provide, which records soft-deletes and revocation events on an append-only audit log.

Retention

We retain transaction records and consent logs for as long as required by law or contract, after which they are anonymized or deleted.

SMS Messaging Disclosure

Effective: April 30, 2026

What you'll receive

If you opt in at the point of sale of a SleetPOS-enabled merchant, you may receive: (a) transactional messages such as digital receipts, refund confirmations, and loyalty point updates, and (b) if you separately opt in to marketing, promotional messages such as discounts and new-product announcements.

Frequency

Transactional messages are sent at the time of each qualifying transaction. Marketing message frequency varies by merchant and is typically 0–4 messages per month.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, charged by your mobile carrier. Sleet Systems does not charge End Customers for SMS.

How to stop

Reply STOP to any message at any time to opt out of all SMS — both transactional and marketing — from that merchant. Reply HELP to receive contact information.

Phone numbers and consent records are handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your phone number to third parties.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Service may not be available on all carriers.
